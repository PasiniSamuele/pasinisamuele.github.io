PHÄNOMENA on Tour has officially started!

---

On Friday, we had the pleasure of presenting our interactive installation “Stay on Track!” during the inauguration in Dietikon, to a large audience—including the President of the Swiss Confederation, Guy Parmelin, marking the official kick-off of the PHÄNOMENA on Tour exhibition.

Our installation allows to modify racetracks and observe how autonomous driving vehicles adapt in real time, exploring the possibilities and the limits of the AI-driven mobility.

You can find some snapshots of PHÄNOMENA on Tour in  <a href = "https://pasinisamuele.github.io/moments/phaenomena/">Moments</a>
